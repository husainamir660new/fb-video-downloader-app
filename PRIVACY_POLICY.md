# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 3, 2026

## 1. Introduction

FB Video Downloader Pro ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Video URLs:** When you paste a Facebook video URL into the App, we process this URL to extract video metadata (title, duration, thumbnail, available resolutions). The URL itself is not stored on our servers.

**Download History:** We store information about videos you download locally on your device, including:
- Video title
- Download date and time
- Quality selected (360p, 480p, 720p)
- File size
- Video ID

**User Preferences:** We store your preferences locally, including:
- Theme selection (light/dark mode)
- Notification settings
- Premium subscription status

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Analytics Data:** We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data:
- App opens and sessions
- Features used
- Download events (quality, success/failure)
- Premium conversion events
- Crashes and errors

**Device Information:**
- Device model and OS version
- App version
- Locale and timezone
- Screen resolution

**Network Information:**
- Connection type (WiFi/cellular)
- Network quality metrics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

1. **Core Functionality**
   - Processing video downloads
   - Displaying download history
   - Saving user preferences

2. **Improvement & Analytics**
   - Understanding how users interact with the App
   - Identifying and fixing bugs
   - Improving performance and user experience
   - Analyzing download trends

3. **Monetization**
   - Displaying personalized ads (via AdMob)
   - Tracking ad performance
   - Processing in-app purchases
   - Managing premium subscriptions

4. **Communication**
   - Sending push notifications about new features
   - Notifying about app updates
   - Responding to support inquiries

5. **Legal Compliance**
   - Complying with applicable laws
   - Enforcing our Terms of Service
   - Protecting against fraud and abuse

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device:
- Download history
- User preferences
- Cached thumbnails

This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains under your control.

### 4.2 Cloud Storage

The following data may be transmitted to our servers:
- Analytics events (anonymized)
- Crash reports
- Ad impressions
- Purchase receipts

### 4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption for all network communications
- Secure storage for sensitive data
- Regular security audits
- Data minimization principles

**Note:** No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

**Local Data:** Stored on your device until you delete the App or manually clear the data.

You can delete your local download history at any time through the App's History screen.

## 7. Your Rights & Choices

### 7.1 Access & Deletion

You have the right to:
- Access your download history
- Delete individual downloads
- Clear all download history
- Uninstall the App to delete all local data

### 7.2 Analytics Opt-Out

You can disable analytics collection through your device settings:
- **Android:** Settings → Apps → FB Video Downloader → Permissions → Disable analytics

### 7.3 Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized ads:
- **Android:** Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad Personalization

### 7.4 Notifications

You can disable push notifications through:
- App Settings → Notifications
- Device Settings → Apps → FB Video Downloader → Notifications
